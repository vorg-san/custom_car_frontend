Welcome to Race2Restore!

This is the landing page of the alpha version!

Race2Restore is a web-based DeFi car auction application that will let its users vote by contributing funds for cars that we restore. Any member can vote with the rest of the community on everything from the car itself, what class of car it becomes, and what parts we use to restore it.

In later Rounds, users can mint their choices as non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on the Rarible protocol which can be later burned towards the final purchase of the restored vehicle.

Please make sure you have a MetaMask wallet setup
